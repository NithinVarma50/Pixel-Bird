About Clumsy Bird

Welcome to Clumsy Bird, a delightful retro-themed Flappy Bird clone crafted with MelonJS!
Take control of our clumsy feathered friend and guide it through challenging pipe obstacles.
How far can you fly before disaster strikes?

This game combines classic arcade gameplay with modern web technology,
featuring smooth animations, retro pixel art, and addictive one-touch controls.
Perfect for quick gaming sessions or competitive high-score challenges!

Developer: Nithin Varma
